## Overview

This repository contains a curated collection of course materials created by me for academic and instructional purposes. It serves as a centralized archive for organizing, maintaining, and sharing educational content developed for teaching, training, and self-study.

The materials are intended to support learning, knowledge dissemination, and academic reuse, while ensuring appropriate attribution to the original author.

## Contents

The repository may include, but is not limited to:
- Lecture notes and slide decks  
- Assignments, exercises, and solutions  
- Reading materials and reference documents  
- Supplementary resources and teaching aids  

Materials may be organized by course, topic, or academic term.
